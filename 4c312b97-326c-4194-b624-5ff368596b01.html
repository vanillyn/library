Description

Hype Prototype is a 3D third-person shooter combination of lowpoly and retrowave / synthwave.


Available on Steam

Introduction

The technological boom has captured humanity, it is difficult to see a person without an electronic device, suddenly people begin to disappear in different places of the planet and get into the virtual world through the screens of their devices, you have to find out what is happening there and find a way back home.

Each level is a separate arena, you need to pass it and get into the portal. Along the way you will meet coins, first aid kits, power-ups and different types of enemies.

Three types of weapons are available to you at any time: revolver, shotgun and rifle. Each weapon has its own characteristics. Endless bullets make the game as dynamic as possible. You just need to aim and press the button.

The quality of passing the level affects the final grade. Your score is entered into the global leaderboard (only in the full version)

Key features

  • ~30 unique levels (5 levels in the demo version)
  • Various items: coins, first aid kits, power-ups and etc
  • A lot of enemies, including special ones (less in the demo version)
  • Endless bullets! Weapons are available at any time
  • Hit the head of enemies and deal critical damage!
  • Leaderboard, compete with other players for the best score! (only in the full version)
  • Three difficulty levels (can be changed at any time)
  • Various player skins (less in the demo version)
  • Singleplayer (it all depends on you)
  • Funny shootouts
  • Juicy visual and sound effects
  • Retro synthwave music
